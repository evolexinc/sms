About Jaipuria Group

Seth Anandram Jaipuria Group of Educational Institutions is a leading conglomerate of 16 K-12 schools, 5 preschools, 2 management institutes and a premier teachers’ training academy in north and central India. The group has a legacy of 78 years in the field of education and presently has the strength of 20,000 students, 15,000 alumni, and 900 educators. What sets us apart is our presence in all verticals of the education sector.

A Brief History

The group’s journey in the education sector began in 1945 with the launch of a commerce

college in Kolkata,which is still operational today and has around 7000 students. In 1974, the group entered the K-12 education domain and has continually been expanding its presence since then. The group’s foray into management education began in 2001. In 2015, the first Little One – The Jaipuria Preschool was launched. Having covered all learning domains from preschools to higher education, the group launched Saamarthya Teachers Training Academy of Research (STTAR) in 2020 to build new-age pedagogical competencies in today’s educators.

K-12 Education

The group has three core schools in the cities of Kanpur, Ghaziabad and Lucknow. Under the partnership model, we have made a lot of progress in the domain of K-12 schools. Starting in the year 2014 with the launch of the first partner school in Farrukhabad, the group has opened partner schools in the cities such as Unnao, Alambagh, Sitarganj, Varanasi, Satna, Mirzapur, Rae Bareli, Dibiyapur, Naini, Nanpara, Amethi, Sidhauli, and Greater Noida among others.

Preschools

The group presently has 2 core playschools in Kakadeo and Ghaziabad, and 3 partner playschools in Vikas Nagar, Jajmau and Swaroop Nagar in Kanpur. 

Management Education

Founded in 2001, Jaipuria Institute of Management has NAAC Grade ‘A’ accreditation and offers an AKTU approved 2-year full-time MBA program. Jaipuria School of Business, established in2008, offers the AICTE-approved PGDM Programme.

Teachers’ Training Academy

Saamarthya Teachers Training Academy of Research (STTAR) has trained tens of thousands of teachers across hundreds of schools in different aspects of modern pedagogy.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Under Corporate Social Responsibility, Seth Anandram Jaipuria Group has established Smriti Bhawans in different holy cities such as Chitrakoot, Vindravan and Haridwar.  Jaipuria group runs a charitable Eye Hospital for under-privileged people in Nawalgarh, Rajasthan, in the name of Seth Anandram Jaipuria Eye Hospital which started in 1943 and has treated more than 2 lakh patients till now. The Ramdarshan monument is one of the attractive places in Chitrakoot run by Jaipuria Group.
